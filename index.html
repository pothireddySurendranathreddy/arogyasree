Dr. Pothireddy Surendranath Reddy

Orthopaedic Surgeon | Arthroscopy & Joint Replacement Specialist

Dr. P. Surendranath Reddy is a highly skilled Orthopaedic Surgeon with over 14 years of clinical, surgical, and academic experience, specializing in Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, and Trauma Surgery.

He completed MBBS from SV Medical College, Tirupati (2006), MS Orthopaedics from Kurnool Medical College (2011), and a prestigious Fellowship in Joint Replacement from Srikara Hospital, Secunderabad (2015).

Currently, he serves as Associate Professor of Orthopaedics at PMRIMS, actively involved in teaching, research, and complex orthopaedic surgeries.
